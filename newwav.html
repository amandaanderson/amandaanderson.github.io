new.wav is a vancouver underground music/art publication that is focused on exploring creative individuals. fresh new waves of creative energy. the name new.wav was inspired by both the new wave music era and .wav, a form of audio file. minimal, modern design was used to create a juxtaposition to the film photography. a happy combination of old and new. keeping one foot in tradition and one searching for that new.wav. embracing analogue and digital without judgment. words in zine by:alex hudson.

services: publication design, art direction, photography, branding.
