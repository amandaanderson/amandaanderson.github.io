anthropology. noun. the study of humankind. in this case: a casual study of a few selected cool creative humanz. everything featured in this little book is subjective and may or may not be able to be backed up by any “logie”. no humans were hurt in this study. at least not physically. questions sourced from various online lists of questions to ask people. answers may or may not be relevant. this is not truly anthropology. but that is okay. to study something that is ever changing is a constant challenge. especially something such as us, who are so disconnected from who we really are. we wear masks to hide our eccentricities.
