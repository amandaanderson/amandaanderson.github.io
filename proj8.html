no big deal record label. designed for justice mclellan. a tiny local record label geared towards putting out good music with a casual vibe. don’t take yourself too seriously and neither will they. because really, it’s no big deal. nbd was hand-lettered to pay homage to their diy ethos. a monospaced font was used to embrace the future of music which is technology. inspiration is a combination of the diy (not quite counter} culture alongside minimalist design. a slightly desaturated magenta was used to represent the casual feel and things not being as they seem. nbdlabel.com
